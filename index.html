Bauhaus93 - Font Demo

Step 1:

Make your project aware of the "Bauhaus93" font 

	@font-face {
		font-family: 'Bauhaus93';
		src: url('assets/fonts/Bauhaus93/Bauhaus93.eot');
		src: local('☺'),
		url('assets/fonts/Bauhaus93/Bauhaus93.woff') format('woff'),
		url('assets/fonts/Bauhaus93/Bauhaus93.ttf') format('truetype'),
		url('assets/fonts/Bauhaus93/Bauhaus93.svg') format('svg');
		font-weight: normal;
		font-style: normal;
	}

Step 2:

.css


	.app-font{
		font-family:'Bauhaus93',Sans-Serif;
		margin:10px auto;
		text-align:center;
		border:1px solid #666;
		padding:10px;
	}

html


	< div class="app-font" style="font-size:25px">
		The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog.
	< /div>

Result:

The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog.
